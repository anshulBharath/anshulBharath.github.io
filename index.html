About

Thank you for stopping by! My name is Anshul Bharath. I am currently a Senior studying Computer Science at the University of St. Thomas. I am currently working at Starkey Labs as a Software Engineer Intern.

I get asked why I wanted to study Computer Science and what made me interested in this field. I like to joke around and say that Tony Stark inspired me, but I can't give all the credit to him. Growing up I had a lot of influence from my parents. They allowed me to see the great possibilities, that a career in Software Development could hold. At the end of the day I got into this field for the love of solving problems.

In my spare time I engage myself in many other activities outside of computer science. Growing up I played tennis and continued to play in college. This last summer, I picked up road biking and enjoy that. I enjoy playing the piano and listening to classical music. Finally, I love reading. I like to keep a few books going and my booklist is never ending!

In the future, I hope to be traveling the world as I work and hopefully documenting my experiences, so stay tuned. Cheers! - Anshul
